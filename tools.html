☽ How Birth Cards Work

To find your birth card, your full birth date is reduced to a number between 1 and 22 through a specific numerological process. This number corresponds to a Major Arcana card — your soul card — which reveals your life's central theme and deepest potential.

When your birth number is between 10 and 22, you have two birth cards: the original number and its reduced single digit. These two cards work in tandem — one reveals your personality archetype, the other your soul archetype. Together they paint the full picture of your spiritual DNA.

Special master numbers (11, 22) are kept as-is rather than further reduced, as they carry amplified spiritual energy.
